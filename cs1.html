branding

task

Create a graphic identity for a client, effectively expressing my own aesthetic style while also satisfying the needs of the client. Develop a greater understanding of empathy in the design process.

idea

Take a logo developed by critique and iteration and apply it to a collection of curated merchandise. Succinctly, create a branded identity.

approach

first

In order to create a compelling product, I found an interview process to be necessary.

I talked to [the client] about logos and brands that she resonated with. I got a feel for what made her tick—her interests, hobbies, and overall identity.

second

Synthesizing all the information I gathered from the interview process, I began ideating through potential merchandise to brand.

I decided to undertake making a template from scratch—I knew I wanted to cite "streetwear" stye as a heavy influence, while challenging my branding skills by keeping in line with [the client]'s appreciation for monochrome.

third

In regard to logo design, I kept [the client]'s preference for geometric design as a priority. By creating several possible directions, [the client] and I were able to select one that was compelling to the both of us.

Drawing from research, I was able to develop a product line that satisfied [the client]'s affinity for minimalism and streetwear fashion.

fourth

Finally, by establishing a feedback loop, I was able to iterate through versions and create a conclusive final product.
